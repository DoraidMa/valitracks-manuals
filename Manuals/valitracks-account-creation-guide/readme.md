    <h1>How to Create an Account on Valitracks</h1>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>1. Receiving the Invitation</h2>
        <p>
            Whether you are a <strong>Project Manager, QA Reviewer, Interviewer, Clinician, or any other user</strong>, your Valitracks account is created when you receive your first invitation.
        </p>
        <p>You will receive this invitation if:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>You were sent a <strong>Purchase Order</strong>, or</li>
            <li>You were <strong>invited to join a workspace</strong>.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>The email contains a link to begin the account creation process.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>2. Signing Up</h2>
        <ol>
            <li>Click the <strong>Sign Up</strong> button in the invitation email.</li>
            <li>Fill in the registration details and click <strong>Submit</strong>.</li>
            <li>Check your inbox for the <strong>verification email</strong>.</li>
            <li><strong>Important:</strong> Use the link inside the verification email to sign in for the first time.</li>
        </ol>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>3. Understanding Workspaces</h2>
        <p>
            After signing in, you will land in your <strong>default workspace</strong>. Workspaces are part of a <strong>company</strong> inside Valitracks. A company can have multiple workspaces, representing different project management teams.
        </p>
